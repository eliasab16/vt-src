SCP quick guide for transfering data to and from Vast.ai
========================================================

Get SSH info:
  vastai ssh-url <instance_id>
  # Returns: ssh -p <port> root@<ip>

SSH into instance:
  ssh -p <port> root@<ip>

Bundle and compress with tar before transfering for faster transfer times:
  tar -czf archive.tar.gz folder/
  scp -P <port> archive.tar.gz root@<ip>:/remote/

Unbundle and decompress:
  tar -xzvf archive.tar.gz

Upload file:
  scp -P <port> /local/file.txt root@<ip>:/remote/path/

Upload folder:
  scp -P <port> -r /local/folder root@<ip>:/remote/path/

Download file:
  scp -P <port> root@<ip>:/remote/file.txt ~/local/path/

Download folder:
  scp -P <port> -r root@<ip>:/remote/folder ~/local/path/
